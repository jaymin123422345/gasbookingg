Mini 5KG Cylinder

5KG Gas Cylinder

One of the main concerns people faced when getting a new gas connection is the long waiting period. The process of applying for a new connection is slow and tedious most of the times. So this very reason, many preferred to buy private gas connections even though it was expensive. To help customers get safe cooking gas in less time, Bharatgas introduced “Mini Connection”. A Bharatgas Mini connection is a 5 Kg LPG cylinder available with or without Domestic Pressure Regulator (DPR) which will be sold at various Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) retail outlets across the country. For now, Bharatgas Mini connection is sold at over 100 petrol stations in the country. Bharatgas will be sold under the Free Trade LPG (FTL) scheme. When a customer buys Bharatgas MIni for the first time, cost of equipment (DPR plus cylinder), administrative charges, and cost of the product will be payable. When a customer books a refill, only the cost of the product will be charged. Bharatgas Mini focuses on making domestic cooking gas easily accessible

How to Apply

To order a Bharatgas Mini, you just have to dial 1800224344 and the cylinder will be delivered within 2 hours. A nominal fee of Rs.25 will be charged when you have Bharatgas delivered to your house. Please note that you will have to provide a proof of identity when you order for a Bharatgas Mini.
Dial-a-Mini

1.You will have to dial the toll-free number   1800224344 to order for a Bharatgas Mini.
2.This number will be available round-the-clock and   you can order anytime.
3.Delivery of Bharatgas Mini will be made from 9 am   to 9 pm.
4.Orders that are placed after 9 pm on any given day   will be delivered the next day after 9 am.
5.Both Bharatgas new connection and refill will be   delivered to the residence of the customer within 2   hours from the time the order is placed.
6.Dial-a-Mini service is available in only 8 cities for   now. Some of the cities are Bangalore, Delhi,   Hyderabad, Mumbai, and Pune.

How to Return

You can return your Bharatgas Mini connection, you can do so and get a refund. Many people return their Bharatgas Mini connection for various reasons like moving out of town or choosing other LPG providers. You can exercise this option in Bharat Petroleum only. Please note that you will be asked to show the proof of purchase when you return the Mini cylinder. If you are returning the Bharatgas Mini within 5 years from the date of purchase, you will get a refund of 50% on the purchase price of the cylinder and pressure regulator. Please note that the cost of gas and taxes will be excluded and is not eligible for a refund.Also, if you return the Bharatgas Mini cylinder after 5 years from the date of purchase, a flat Rs.100 will be given to the customer.
